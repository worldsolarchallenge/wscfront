Who's in front?

It is difficult to tell who is winning when teams were driving under different conditions in different parts of the world. However, we can say that team A dominates team B if its average driving speed is higher and it uses less energy per distance travelled.

The following graph shows average driving speed and energy consumption for each of the teams. A team dominates another if it is lower and to the right.
